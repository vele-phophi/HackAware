Why Passwords Matter

Passwords are the first line of defense against unauthorized access to your accounts and data. Weak or reused passwords make it easier for attackers to break in.

Common Mistakes

  • Using the same password across multiple sites
  • Choosing short or simple passwords (e.g., "123456", "password")
  • Sharing passwords with others
  • Not updating passwords regularly

How to Create Strong Passwords

  • Use at least 12 characters
  • Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid personal info like names or birthdays
  • Use passphrases (e.g., "BlueTiger$Eats!Pizza")
