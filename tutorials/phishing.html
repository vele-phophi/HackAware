What is Phishing?

Common Signs of Phishing

  • Urgent or threatening language ("Your account will be suspended!")
  • Suspicious links or email addresses
  • Requests for personal or financial information
  • Spelling and grammar mistakes

How to Stay Safe

  • Use multi-factor authentication

Real-World Example

Imagine receiving an email from "support@yourbank-secure.com" asking you to confirm your password. The domain looks legit, but it's a fake. Always double-check!
